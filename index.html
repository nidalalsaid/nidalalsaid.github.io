Selected Books

Advanced Deep Learning Architectures & Techniques (Kindle Edition)
Dr. Betshrine Rachel Jibinsingh · Dr. Anurag Shrivastava · Dr. Nidal Al Said
Machine learning and deep learning have rapidly evolved, creating vast opportunities for engineering students but lacking unified, structured study material that covers core concepts, applications, and emerging research. This book bridges that gap by presenting foundational ideas alongside real-world applications, empowering learners to build practical ML solutions across diverse domains.
Publisher Amazon
Intelligent Web Mining: Discovering Patterns in Online Data
Dr. Nidal Al Said
This book offers a structured, comprehensive introduction to web mining by covering its three core pillars—web content, structure, and usage mining—along with modern tools and frameworks. It provides an integrated perspective on analyzing user behavior, hyperlink patterns, and social media data, while also addressing scalable mining approaches in cloud environments.

Selected Publications

Does Data Privacy Influence Digital Marketing? The Mediating Role of AI-Driven Trust
Al Said, N. (2024). International Journal of Data and Network Science, 8, 1–10.
This research examines how AI-driven trust mechanisms mediate the negative impact of data privacy concerns on digital marketing effectiveness among ZAIN Telecom employees. The findings show that robust AI trust systems are essential for mitigating privacy risks and sustaining competitive advantage.
View (DOI)
A Hybrid GAN-CNN Network with Attention Mechanism for Detecting Fake Profile Images in Microblogs
Al Said, N. (2025). Ingénierie des Systèmes d’Information, 30(6), 1145–1152.
This work develops the “GAMN” model, a hybrid GAN-CNN architecture using a Gram matrix and self-attention to detect synthetic profile images. The model achieved 89.27% accuracy, outperforming ResNet and Co-detect benchmarks.
