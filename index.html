Selected Publications

Does data privacy influence digital marketing? The mediating role of AI-driven trust
Al Said, N. (2024). International Journal of Data and Network Science, 8, 1–10.
This research examines how AI-driven trust mechanisms mediate the negative impact of data privacy concerns on digital marketing effectiveness among ZAIN Telecom employees. The findings reveal that robust AI trust systems are essential for mitigating privacy risks and securing a sustainable competitive advantage.
View (DOI)
A hybrid GAN-CNN network with attention mechanism for detecting fake profile images in microblogs
Al Said, N. (2025). Ingénierie des Systèmes d’Information, 30(6), 1145–1152.
This research develops the “GAMN” model, a hybrid GAN-CNN architecture that utilizes a Gram matrix and self-attention mechanisms to detect synthetic profile images. By prioritizing specific facial features, the model achieved 89.27% accuracy, significantly outperforming ResNet and Co-detect benchmarks.
