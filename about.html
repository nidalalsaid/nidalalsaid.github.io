Dr. Nidal Al Said

Dr. Nidal Al Said is an Associate Professor of Information Technology with over two decades of academic, research, and leadership experience across higher education institutions in the Middle East and Europe. His work integrates Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Human–Computer Interaction, Data Analytics, and Smart Digital Systems within interdisciplinary academic environments.

His academic profile combines research excellence, curriculum innovation, institutional effectiveness leadership, and strategic accreditation expertise—positioning him as both a scholar and an academic developer capable of contributing at institutional, college, and program levels.

Research and Scholarly Contribution

Dr. Nidal Al Said’s research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Human–AI Interaction, Intelligent Information Systems, Data Mining, Smart Applications, and AI-driven analytics for education, media, and organizational environments. His work emphasizes not only algorithmic performance but also usability, interpretability, and measurable institutional impact.

He has published extensively in reputable peer-reviewed journals, international conferences, and edited academic volumes. His publications span AI-based predictive modeling, hybrid intelligent systems, data-driven decision support frameworks, and smart digital transformation strategies. His scholarship reflects methodological rigor, applied relevance, and interdisciplinary integration.

Books, Intellectual Property, and Innovation

In addition to journal publications, Dr. Nidal Al Said is the author and co-author of multiple academic books addressing Artificial Intelligence, intelligent systems, digital transformation, and smart analytics. His books aim to translate complex technologies into structured, teachable, and application-oriented knowledge.
